Cleaner tiles and brighter grout lines

JW Carpet Care offers tile and grout cleaning for kitchens, hallways, utility rooms, bathrooms, toilets and suitable commercial tiled floors. The work focuses on removing built-up soil from the surface and improving the look of grout lines without pretending every stained grout line can be made brand new.

Kitchen and hallway tilesUseful for greasy film, dull traffic lanes and everyday soil that has built up over time.
Grout line cleaningTargeted agitation helps lift dirt from the lines where mops often struggle.
Commercial tiled floorsFor toilets, entrances, staff areas and customer-facing floors that need to look clean and cared for.
A test area or inspection may be recommended where grout is damaged, heavily stained, poorly sealed or where the floor has previous coatings. This keeps the advice honest and avoids overpromising.
